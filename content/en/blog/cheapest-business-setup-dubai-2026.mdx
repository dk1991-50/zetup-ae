# Cheapest Business Setup in Dubai 2026 — What Things Actually Cost

**Answer Capsule:**
The cheapest way to set up a business in Dubai in 2026 is the Dubai Instant Licence (from AED 5,000 for select activities). Budget free zones like IFZA and Meydan offer packages from AED 12,000–15,000. A standard mainland LLC costs AED 28,000–55,000 in Year 1. Advertised "from AED 5,500" packages typically exclude essential costs that double the real price.

Let me be direct: most "cheapest business setup" articles are marketing content from formation companies advertising misleadingly low prices. This post gives you the actual numbers.

## The Real Options, Ranked by Cost

**Option 1: Dubai Instant Licence — From AED 5,000**
The genuinely cheapest option. A real DET mainland licence issued in 5–10 minutes. Covers select activities. No office required in Year 1. Limitation: not all activities available, and you need an office for visa processing.

**Option 2: Budget Free Zone — AED 12,000–20,000**
Zones like IFZA, Meydan, and RAKEZ offer all-inclusive packages. Good for freelancers and international businesses. Limitation: cannot trade on UAE mainland.

**Option 3: Established Free Zone — AED 25,000–50,000**
DMCC, JAFZA, DIC. Better infrastructure and reputation, but higher cost. Suitable for companies needing zone-specific advantages.

**Option 4: Mainland LLC — AED 28,000–55,000**
Full trading rights, government contract eligibility, flexible visa allocation. Higher cost justified by operational flexibility.

## The Hidden Cost Problem

When you see "business setup from AED 5,500" in an ad, here is what is typically excluded: office registration (AED 5,000–15,000), establishment card (AED 2,000), MOA notarisation (AED 2,000–5,000), visa processing (AED 3,500–6,000 each), and various "processing fees." The real cost is always 2–4x the advertised price.
