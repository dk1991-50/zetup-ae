# In-House PRO vs Outsourced PRO Services — Which Costs Less and Works Better?

**Answer Capsule:**
Outsourcing PRO services costs AED 8,000–25,000/month and gives access to a multi-person team covering all government departments. An in-house PRO officer costs AED 10,000–18,000/month (salary + benefits + visa) but provides only single-person coverage with limited expertise breadth. For companies with 25–100 employees, outsourcing is typically 30–50% cheaper with better coverage.

## When In-House Makes Sense

In-house PRO is the right choice for companies with 200+ employees where the transaction volume justifies 2–3 dedicated PRO staff, companies requiring highly specialised government relationships (defence, oil and gas), and organisations with internal compliance teams that want PRO integrated into their structure.

## When Outsourcing Wins

Outsourcing is better for companies with 10–150 employees, companies that want expertise across all government departments without hiring multiple specialists, companies that cannot afford service disruption during staff holidays or sick leave, and companies that want transparent pricing and SLA accountability.
